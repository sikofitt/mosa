Drupal 25th Anniversary Gala!

March 24th 7 pm to 10 pm CST (World Time Buddy)

610 S Michigan Ave
Chicago, IL 60605, USA

For a quarter-century, Drupal has been more than just a software project; it has been a launchpad for careers, a place for lifelong friendships, and a powerful engine driving the Open Web.

To celebrate the 25th anniversary of Drupal during DrupalCon Chicago, we are coming together as one unified community for a night of connection, history, and fun.

We’ll have food, drinks, music, and a few surprises (yes, there will be a Drupal 25th Anniversary beer) as we honor the past, celebrate the present, and shape our future.

  • Move, mingle, and graze through curated culinary stations throughout the evening
  • Dress code is “Drupal Fancy” — gowns, black tie, and Drupal tees and hoodies are equally welcome.
  • Music, dancing, and a night-long celebration of our shared success.
  • A dedicated, lower-volume sanctuary for deep conversation, catching up with old friends, and sharing community stories.
  • Inspiring milestone moments honoring the past, present, and exciting future of Drupal.
